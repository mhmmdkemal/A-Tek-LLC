About Me

I am interested in doing research on sustainable agriculture with a focus in Africa. This is essentially the topic of study that would be used for my PHD dissertation. As we all know, much of the citizens in the independent countries of Africa sustain themselves through agricultural means. What does this mean to me? Well, I want to help people all over the world but I want to focus on Africa. This brings the question "How can I best help these people?" This comes down to what will make a great impact on their lives; this led me to focus on agriculture and how to make it flourish while making it more sustainable.
